Save our home

Water pollution is a serious problem that we cause in
many ways, such as chemical pollution, physical
pollution, noise pollution, and microplastics. Sadly, many
countries are not taking action to solve this issue.
To make a change, we started a project called "Y-TECH".
Our aim is to remove excess CO2 from water.
While some animals can live with plastic, marine life avoids
areas with high CO2 levels. Microorganisms cannot
survive there either. High pH levels harm corals, which
are essential homes for many marine animals. Our
project will track and show the water’s condition .
